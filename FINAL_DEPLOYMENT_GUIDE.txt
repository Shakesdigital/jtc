┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ OPTION 1: NETLIFY DRAG & DROP (EASIEST - RECOMMENDED) ⭐          │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Step 1: OPEN TWO WINDOWS
  ▸ Window 1: Open File Explorer (Windows Key + E)
    → Navigate to: D:\Jinja Town Church Work\JTCapp
    → You'll see the "build" folder

  ▸ Window 2: Open browser
    → Go to: https://app.netlify.com/
    → Log in to your Netlify account

Step 2: IN NETLIFY
  ▸ Click on your site (jtc.shakesdigital.com or similar)
  ▸ Click "Deploys" tab at the top
  ▸ Scroll down to see drag & drop box
    (It says: "Need to update your site? Drag and drop your site 
     output folder here")

Step 3: DRAG & DROP
  ▸ From File Explorer (Window 1):
    → Click and HOLD the "build" folder
    → DRAG it to your browser (Window 2)
    → DROP it in the Netlify drop zone

Step 4: WAIT FOR UPLOAD
  ▸ Upload will take 5-10 minutes (259MB)
  ▸ You'll see a progress bar
  ▸ DON'T CLOSE the browser
  ▸ Wait until you see "Site is live" ✅

Step 5: TEST
  ▸ Visit your site: jtc.shakesdigital.com/sermons/archive
  ▸ Click "Listen" on "Finding Your Soulmate"
  ▸ Audio should play! 🎵


┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ OPTION 2: NETLIFY CLI (FOR COMMAND LINE USERS)                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Open Command Prompt in: D:\Jinja Town Church Work\JTCapp

Then run these commands ONE AT A TIME:

1. Install Netlify CLI (first time only):
   npm install -g netlify-cli

2. Login to Netlify:
   netlify login
   (Browser will open, click "Authorize")

3. Deploy:
   netlify deploy --prod --dir=build

4. Wait 5-10 minutes for upload
5. You'll get a URL when done!


┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ OPTION 3: IF NETLIFY TIMES OUT (BACKUP PLAN)                       │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

If upload times out, deploy without audio first:

1. Remove audio temporarily:
   rm -rf build/sermons/audio

2. Deploy the lighter build (without audio)

3. Then manually upload JUST the audio files via FTP:
   From: D:\Jinja Town Church Work\JTCapp\public\sermons\audio
   To: Your server /public/sermons/audio/
